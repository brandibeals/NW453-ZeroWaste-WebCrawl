AMBASSADORS

You, as an ambassador, will be part of the mission of being part of a Zero Waste Youth , being a youth responsible for your waste in addition to convey the concept to other youths, making them also part of the Zero Waste Youth Movement.

But how? Simple. Use your creativity. We have some ideas, but that does not mean you can not create new innovative ways to tell people.

We work changing people’s minds and this is not an easy task, but if it were easy it would be for us, right?

You can:

  • Create a Zero Waste Youth Meeting in your city / country
  • Have a blog where you will updated about Zero Waste in your city / country, current status, cool initiatives and events, news, etc.
  • Create events like Zero Waste Rock Concert, Zero Waste Marches, Zero Waste Racing / Hiking, “Days of Exchange”
  • Create a local committee that will work with you in this movement.
