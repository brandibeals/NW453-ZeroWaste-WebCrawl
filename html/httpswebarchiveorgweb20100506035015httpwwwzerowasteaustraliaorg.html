Zero Waste Australia

Zero Waste Australia is part of an international movement that aims to promote healthy community without waste.

We advocate meeting human needs through infrastructure design and resource use that works in harmony with natural systems. In nature, the output from every natural process provides an input for another. There is no waste.

The members of Zero Waste Australia are leaders in net positive systems design.
