Carbon Labels

A Leap Forward for Zero Emissions

Allbirds

Allbirds, a sustainable shoe company, is taking a leap forward with labeling every item it manufactures with its carbon footprint. Like how packaged food has a nutrition label, the carbon label will represent the CO2 emitted to create it.

And, until they reach their zero goal, the company will invest in offsets to fund projects that neutralize its footprint.
